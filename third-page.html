Your Mission

You are a human that has been sent into the void. Your mission is to not get messed up by them damn aliens. Luckily, your trustworthy ship has joined in your side in the fight that no one will see, (other than you player(s)) as this is space, apart from your enemies. Which will taste lasers! Nonetheless, you will fly around using the up and down keys while attacking aliens which have inhabited the planets you come across. If your looking for a reason as to why you're destroying them , well ... lets just say you're a crazy lunatic who managed to swindled a pilot out of their keys after being exposed to anti-alien propaganda.
